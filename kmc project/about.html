Kathmandu Model College alias KMC is a leading academic institution committed to academic par excellence along with the overall development of the students. We believe that every student possesses inimitable caliber and aptitude in the latent form. Our seasoned teaching faculties help explore to give an outlet to your hidden potentials through pragmatic and learner centered approach in the decent ambiance. Rather than lofty and monotonous lengthy lectures our students spend fair amount of time in group work pair work and researches. We ensure a supportive and caring milieu and deeply transformative experience, intellectually, socially, and personally. At KMC, you will have the opportunity to pursue your intellectual curiosity and engage with academics at the forefront of cutting-edge research to contribute to solving some of our global challenges.
